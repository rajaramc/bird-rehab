Every spring storm knocks nests out of trees, and every one of those nests
seems to find its way here. This week alone: four robins, three house
finches, two chickadees, and two mourning doves.

Nestlings eat every 30 minutes from sunrise to sunset. That's not a figure
of speech — it's an alarm going off 28 times a day.

If you've found a baby bird, please check the [Found a bird?](/found-a-bird)
page first. Fully feathered fledglings on the ground are usually fine and
their parents are watching. The naked, pink, closed-eye ones are the true
emergencies.

**What we need most right now:** mealworms, paper towels, and heating pads
without auto-shutoff. The [donate page](/donate) has the details.
