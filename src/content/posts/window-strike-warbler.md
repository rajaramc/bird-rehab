A homeowner in Richfield heard the thud, found the warbler stunned on her
deck, and did everything right: dark box, no food, quick phone call.

After 48 hours of observation for head trauma, he was flying circles
around the songbird room and clearly done with our hospitality. Released
in the same yard — with new window decals installed.

**The easy fix:** exterior window dots or screens break up reflections
that birds read as open sky. A $15 roll of decals genuinely saves lives.
